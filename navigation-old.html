NAVIGATION

The navigation header should appear at the top of every PACE wizard. It contains the title of the page, version number, AT&T logo, and navigation to other PACE-related links.

The header has the following styling:

Width: 100%;
Height: 60px;
Background: #1EAEE4;
Color: White;
